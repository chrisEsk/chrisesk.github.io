# Introduction

I've worked in various kinds of projects such as web applications, video games, multi-platform mobile applications for iOS and Android and Chromecast applications.

I had the privilege to be the tech lead for El Artesano App, which is a multi-platform social network thats all about the artisan beer scene in Costa Rica, and expanding to other countries.

As tech lead of a small team of developers, I have years of experience following agile methodologies, optimizing processes within the agile model to suit the needs of the team to become more efficient.

I am a proactive person thats constantly experimenting with new tech, so I'm generally the guy that explores uncharted territories. A good example of this is a Chromecast application I developed for El Artesano App, where I had to self-taught how to develop such app.

I generally prefer the programming part of my job above any other phase within software development, but I am capable of executing any process.

Another skill I have is graphic design, especially for building UX, frontend interfaces.

I believe excellence can only be achieved if you're passionate about what you're doing. This is something the users can tell when using your product, and I am grateful to say that I found my passion for software and technology from an early age.
